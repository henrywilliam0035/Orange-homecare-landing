Frequently Asked Questions

How does delivery work?

We offer free delivery in Lagos and Kano. For other locations, please contact us for delivery arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash on delivery for Lagos and Kano orders.

How long does a refill last?

A 1L refill typically lasts 2-4 weeks for a small household. The 3L family size lasts 6-8 weeks for average families.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes! There are no long-term commitments. You can pause or cancel your refill service anytime.
