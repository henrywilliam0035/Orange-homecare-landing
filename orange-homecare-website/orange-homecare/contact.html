Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Lagos: 24-48 hours. Other cities: 3-5 business days. We'll confirm delivery time when you place your order.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer and payment on delivery (Lagos only). Payment details are shared via WhatsApp after you place your order.

Is Gleam safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our formula is dermatologist-friendly and non-toxic. It's designed to be tough on grease but gentle on hands.

How long does one bottle last?

A 500ml bottle typically lasts 4-6 weeks for a family of 4, depending on usage.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes! We deliver to all states in Nigeria. Free delivery available in Lagos for qualifying orders.

How do refills work?

Keep your original bottle and order our refill pouches. Simply pour the refill into your bottle - easy, eco-friendly, and saves you money!
